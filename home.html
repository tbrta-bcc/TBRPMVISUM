About

The TBRPM v9.3 has been developed for the planning activities under the Regional Transportation Analysis (RTA) for the Tampa Bay Area. The RTA is the cooperative and combined planning efforts of the Florida Department of Transportation (FDOT) District Seven (D7) and the Metropolitan Planning Organizations (MPOs) in the Tampa Bay Area. The study area for the TBRPM corresponds with the jurisdiction of the FDOT D7 and includes Hillsborough, Pinellas, Pasco, Hernando, and Citrus Counties of Florida.

Model Information

  • Model Version: TBRPM v9.3
  • Released Date: 08/01/2023(should be updated)
  • Travel Forecast Process: Trip Generation, Trip Distribution, Mode Choice, Trip Assignment
  • Travel Forecast Tool: PTV VISUM

Model Download

Tampa Bay Regional Planning Model

  • 1. Download model from Google Drive
  • 2. Save the file under “C:\FSUTMS\D7”
  • 3. Right-click the file and click “Extract All”
  • 4. Extract the zip file to “C:\FSUTMS\D7\TBRPM v9.3”
  • 5. Then model is ready under “C:\FSUTMS\D7\TBRPM v9.3”
